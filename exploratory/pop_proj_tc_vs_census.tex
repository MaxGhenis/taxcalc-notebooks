    \section{Population projections: taxcalc vs
Census}\label{population-projections-taxcalc-vs-census}

This compares population totals between taxcalc and
\href{https://www.census.gov/data/datasets/2017/demo/popproj/2017-popproj.html}{Census
projections} for the following fields: * \texttt{XTOT} (total
population) * \texttt{nu05} (under 5) * \texttt{nu13} (under 13) *
\texttt{n24} (children eligible for the Child Tax Credit, of which the
primary condition is being under age 17) * \texttt{nu18} (under 18) *
\texttt{n1820} (18-20) * \texttt{n21} (21 or older)

\emph{Data: CPS \textbar{} Tax years: 2014-2018 \textbar{} Type: Static
\textbar{} Author: Max Ghenis \textbar{} Date run: 2018-03-20}
